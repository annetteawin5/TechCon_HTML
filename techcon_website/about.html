History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate technologists who envisioned a platform for sharing groundbreaking ideas and fostering collaboration within the tech community. What started as a small gathering of local enthusiasts quickly grew into an international phenomenon, attracting thousands of attendees and industry leaders from across the globe.

Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of showcasing revolutionary technologies, from the early days of mobile computing to the current era of artificial intelligence and blockchain. Our commitment to innovation and knowledge exchange has remained steadfast, making us a pivotal event in the annual tech calendar.

Mission

Our mission at TechCon 2024 is to inspire, educate, and connect the global technology community. We strive to create an inclusive environment where ideas flourish, partnerships are forged, and the future of technology is shaped.

We are dedicated to:

  • **Showcasing Cutting-Edge Innovations:** Bringing together the brightest minds and the latest technologies.
  • **Fostering Knowledge Exchange:** Providing a platform for deep dives into critical tech trends and challenges.
  • **Promoting Diversity and Inclusion:** Ensuring that TechCon is a welcoming space for all backgrounds and perspectives.
  • **Empowering Future Leaders:** Inspiring the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs.

Past Speakers

We are proud to have hosted some of the most influential figures in the tech industry. Here are a few notable speakers from previous TechCon events:

Dr. Anya Sharma

Dr. Anya Sharma, a renowned quantum computing physicist, captivated the audience at TechCon 2022 with her insights into the future of computational power. Her work has significantly contributed to the advancement of quantum algorithms and their real-world applications.

Marcus "Mav" Veltri

Marcus Veltri, widely known as "Mav" in the blockchain community, delivered a powerful keynote at TechCon 2021 on decentralized finance (DeFi) and its impact on traditional banking systems. He is the founder of several successful blockchain startups.

Sarah Chen

Sarah Chen, a celebrated cybersecurity strategist, spoke at TechCon 2019 about the evolving landscape of cyber threats and the importance of proactive security measures. Her expertise lies in protecting critical infrastructure from sophisticated attacks.
